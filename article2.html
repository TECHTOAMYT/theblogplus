Cybersecurity in the Digital Age: Stay Safe Online

With the increasing reliance on digital platforms, cybersecurity has become a top priority. Data breaches, phishing attacks, and ransomware are just a few of the threats that individuals and businesses face daily.

Top Cybersecurity Threats

  1. Phishing: Fraudulent emails or messages designed to steal personal information.
  2. Ransomware: Malware that encrypts data until a ransom is paid.
  3. Data Breaches: Unauthorized access to sensitive information.
  4. Social Engineering: Manipulating individuals into revealing confidential information.

Best Practices for Staying Safe

  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Keep software and systems up to date.
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails and links.
  • Invest in a reliable antivirus and firewall.
