Understanding Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are two of the most groundbreaking technologies shaping the future. This article explores the basics, applications, and implications of AI and ML.

1. What is AI?

AI refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. AI is used in applications ranging from voice assistants like Siri to autonomous vehicles.

2. Machine Learning: A Subset of AI

Machine Learning, a subset of AI, enables computers to learn from data and improve their performance over time without being explicitly programmed.

Example Program: Simple Linear Regression in Python

                    
                        import numpy as np
                        from sklearn.linear_model import LinearRegression

                        # Data: Hours studied vs Marks obtained
                        X = np.array([1, 2, 3, 4, 5]).reshape(-1, 1)
                        y = np.array([1, 2, 2, 3, 3])

                        # Model
                        model = LinearRegression()
                        model.fit(X, y)

                        # Predict
                        print(model.predict([[6]]))  # Predict for 6 hours

3. Applications of AI and ML

From healthcare and finance to entertainment and security, AI and ML have vast applications that continue to revolutionize industries.
